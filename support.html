表达，而不只是修图

dooo0t 是一款面向创作的视觉表达 App，提供一组小而独立、可组合的图像工具，让日常照片转化为具有风格的作品。

功能介绍
◈

拼贴与结构重组

通过拼贴和结构重组，快速生成具有个人气质的图像表达，让普通照片拥有独特视觉语言。

◎

材质变化

丰富的材质处理选项，赋予图像不同的质感与氛围，让每一张作品都有属于自己的触感。

⊞

可组合的工具集

每一个功能简洁直接，但彼此之间可以连续组合，形成属于自己的创作方式与工作流。

◱

风格保存与复用

保存和复用自己的风格结构，让灵感不流失，创作方式可以随时延续和迭代。

◉

浏览与再创作

浏览他人的作品并进行再创作，让表达不断延展，在社区中找到共鸣与启发。

◌

轻量与克制

dooo0t 更关注"如何表达"，而不是"如何修图"，在轻量与克制中构建持续生长的视觉世界。

Express yourself, not just edit

dooo0t is a visual expression app built for creators. It offers a set of small, independent, and composable image tools that transform everyday photos into styled works.

Features
◈

Collage & Restructuring

Quickly generate images with a personal character through collage and structural reorganization, giving ordinary photos a distinct visual language.

◎

Texture & Material

Rich texture options give your images different qualities and atmospheres, ensuring every piece has its own tactile feel.

⊞

Composable Toolset

Each tool is simple and direct, yet they can be combined in sequence — forming your own creative workflow and working style.

◱

Save & Reuse Styles

Save and reuse your style structures so inspiration is never lost, and your creative approach can evolve and continue at any time.

◉

Browse & Recreate

Browse others' works and recreate from them, extending your expression further and finding resonance and inspiration in the community.

◌

Lightweight & Restrained

dooo0t focuses on "how to express" rather than "how to retouch" — building a continuously growing visual world through lightness and restraint.
